How to Use the Timezone Converter

1

Enter Your Current Time

Type your current local time in the format HH:MM (24-hour format) or HH:MM AM/PM (12-hour format). For example: "14:30" or "2:30 PM"

2

Select Source Timezone

Choose the continent and then the specific location of your current timezone. This tells the converter where you are located.

3

Select Destination Timezone

Choose the continent and location where you want to know the equivalent time. This is where you want to convert your time to.

4

Get Your Result

Click "Submit" to see the converted time in both 24-hour and 12-hour formats for your destination timezone.

Available Timezones

Our timezone converter uses the World Time API and supports timezones across all continents.

Tips & Information

Time Format Support
  • 24-hour format: 14:30, 09:15, 23:45
  • 12-hour format: 2:30 PM, 9:15 AM, 11:45 PM
  • Both formats are accepted and converted
Daylight Saving Time
  • Automatically handled by World Time API
  • Updates based on current date and location
  • No manual adjustment needed
Supported Regions
  • Africa - All major African cities
  • America - North, Central, and South America
  • Asia - All Asian countries and cities
  • Europe - All European countries
  • Australia - Australian cities and territories
  • Pacific - Pacific islands and territories
  • Atlantic - Atlantic islands and regions
  • Antarctica - Antarctic research stations
Common Examples
  • New York: America/New_York
  • London: Europe/London
  • Tokyo: Asia/Tokyo
  • Sydney: Australia/Sydney
  • Dubai: Asia/Dubai
  • Los Angeles: America/Los_Angeles
